Printer Setup

Page SizeA4 (210 × 297 mm) for invoices / 80mm for thermal bills

MarginsLeft: 10mm, Right: 10mm, Top: 10mm, Bottom: 10mm

ScalingActual Size (100%) - Do not fit to page / Do not scale

Browser Print SettingsHeaders & Footers: Off • Background Graphics: On • Print Background Colors: On

Tip: Always preview before printing and use a test print when changing settings.
⚠

Common Print Issues

Extra blank page after invoiceAdjust bottom margin and uncheck “Fit to page”.

Logo or image not printingEnable background graphics and allow images.

Thermal width mismatchSelect 80mm paper size and 72mm content width.

Content cut or misalignedCheck margins, reduce font size and test again.
